AIR POLUTION

WHAT IS IT?

Air pollution is the contamination of an outdoor or indoor environment, due to some chemical or biological agents that modifies the natural characteristics of the air.

CONSEQUENCES

Humans due to air polution, are more likely to develop short term problems, such as pneumonia and bronchite. And Long term problems like lung cancer and heart diseases.

Most of the air pollution is caused by Carbon Dioxide, this substance is also the main factor when it comes to Global warming, which is connected to the raise of sea levels and many more.

SOLUTIONS

Making the appliance of filters in the factories chimneys mandatory, and start slowly transitating from combustion cars to sustainable ones or start using public transports.

Stop using pesticides on farms, and start using more biologic friendly ways of managing livestock
